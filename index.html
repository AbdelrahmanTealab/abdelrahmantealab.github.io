Virtual Martial Arts Stance Trainer using EEG and Motion Capture

Practical Thesis

Objective
The objective of solving the availability problem in martial arts is not only to provide more availability for the trainees, but also to provide better accuracy and feedback. The method of how this solution would be implemented is by using two main sensors, EEG and a MOCAP sensor in order to analyse body postures and states of mind. Achievements so far are the possibility to capture the traineeâ€™s body posture and measure angles between them as well as recording the mind state and providing feedback for each of the two aspects.
Description
Virtual Martial Arts coach for people that can't have physical access to martial art gyms.

why MOCAP...

  • Realtime image to bones (stick figures).
  • Applying the Dot Product equation to read the bones as vectors.
  • Acquiring the angles from the Dot Product equation between 2 vectors.
  • Compare the angles of the real time recording to a pre-set data for each stance.
  • Provide feedback accordingly.

why EEG...

  • Real time brainwave monitoring.
  • Brainwave signals translation into Attention and Meditation.
  • Comparing Attention and Meditation levels to the required state.
  • Providing feedback about mental state per posture.
